## The Genre×Name Formula

### MILF Genre (n=16)
- **Mean syllables:** 2.12 (41% shorter than mainstream 2.59)
- **Mean career:** 21.4 years (longest of all genres)
- **Formula:** Accessibility + Relatability → Short Names
- **Examples:** Lisa Ann, Julia Ann, Brandi Love, Sara Jay
- **Mechanism:** Genre markets on "relatable mature woman" → names reflect accessibility

### Petite Genre (n=13)
- **Mean syllables:** 2.08 (very short)
- **Formula:** Youthful + Accessible → Short Names  
- **Mechanism:** Genre positioning through name simplicity

### Gonzo Genre (n=26)
- **Mean syllables:** 2.42 (shorter than mainstream)
- **Formula:** Edgy + Memorable → Moderate brevity
- **Mechanism:** Performance intensity, names slightly optimized

### Mainstream Genre (n=141)
- **Mean syllables:** 2.59 (longest)
- **Formula:** No clear optimization (saturated market)
- **Mechanism:** Broad category, names don't differentiate
