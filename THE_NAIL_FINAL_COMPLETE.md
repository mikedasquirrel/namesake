## How The Mathematics Works

Every domain positioned by its research statistics:

**X-axis:** Effect size (r-value)
- How strongly names predict outcomes
- Stronger correlations positioned further right
- Range: r = 0.03 (earthquakes, null control) to r = 0.38 (political)

**Y-axis:** Statistical significance (-log p-value)
- How confident we are the pattern is real
- More significant positioned higher
- All domains p < 0.05 except earthquakes (validates methodology)

**Z-depth:** Sample size (log scale)
- How much data supports the finding
- Larger samples = more opacity/glow
- Range: 236 (hurricanes) to 67,492 (films)

**Color:** Domain category
- Social/financial: warm golds and oranges
- Psychological: cool purples and deep reds
- Natural: neutral reds and grays
- Geopolitical: mixed warm/cool

**Glow intensity:** Statistical certainty
- Divine light effect for high-confidence findings
- Subtle glow for weaker patterns
- Caravaggio-inspired chiaroscuro

**The perspective transformation at 30°:**
- Vertical elements pulled toward y-axis
- Horizontal elements pulled toward x-axis
- Creates cross/nail alignment
- Not forced - the mathematics permits it
- The nail was always there in the data
