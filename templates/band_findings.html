Why Temporal Analysis Matters

Unlike crypto (where memorability is always negative) or MTG (where commas always help), band names don't have a universal formula. What worked in 1970 (epic fantasy names, prog rock complexity) fails in 2010 (minimalist indie, abstract concepts).

The breakthrough: We must analyze decade-by-decade. Each era has its own aesthetic, shaped by media technology, cultural movements, and genre evolution. The "Beatles formula" is historically specific, not universally applicable.
