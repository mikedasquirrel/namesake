The Research Question

Nominative determinism is the hypothesis that names influence outcomes. This study tests whether linguistic properties of names‚Äîacross cryptocurrencies, hurricanes, medications, bands, players, and more‚Äîsystematically correlate with measurable outcomes.

Central Finding: Nominative patterns exist but vary dramatically by sphere. Memorability predicts positive outcomes in MTG but negative in crypto. Phonetic harshness increases hurricane casualties by 27%. Each domain reveals distinct linguistic-outcome relationships.

The AI Age Challenge

We live in an era where algorithms can extract correlations from any dataset. The distinction between pattern discovery and pattern fishing lies in reproducibility. If linguistic effects are genuine, they should manifest across independent datasets.

Our Approach
