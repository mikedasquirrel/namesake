How We Score Player Names

Harshness Score

harshness = (plosive_count + fricative_count) / total_consonants // Examples: Kobe: /K/ /B/ (2 harsh) ‚Üí 100/100 Shaq: /SH/ /K/ (2 harsh) ‚Üí 100/100 Jordan: /J/ /D/ (2 harsh) ‚Üí 85/100
