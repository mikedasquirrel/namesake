Key Properties

Non-Linear Interactions

Effects are not additive. Harshness √ó Metal Genre creates synergy (+2.8√ó longevity), but Harshness √ó Pop creates penalty. Context modulates phonetic impact.

Threshold Effects

Benefits exhibit gating: crypto names need memorability > 80 to access high-performance cluster. Below threshold, no effect. This explains weak linear correlations.

Saturation Dynamics

As optimal patterns spread, advantages decay. First movers with "perfect" names gain edge, but imitation dilutes signal. Requires continuous linguistic innovation.

Fundamental Dominance

Linguistic quality is necessary but insufficient. Names enable competitive positioning (cohort membership) but don't override fundamental quality. Effect sizes are modest (10-30%).
