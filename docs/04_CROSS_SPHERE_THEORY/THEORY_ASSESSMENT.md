## Limitations & Caveats

### Current Limitations

1. **Domain Data Scarcity**: 26 sales insufficient for strong conclusions
2. **Survivorship Bias**: Only analyzing existing/successful assets
3. **Correlation â‰  Causation**: Names may correlate with other success factors
4. **Time Period**: Data from 2020-2025 bull market
5. **Geographic**: Primarily English-language names
6. **Market Specific**: Crypto is unique market (speculation, technology)

### Methodological Concerns

1. **Multiple Testing**: 13 patterns discovered, some may be false positives
2. **Overfitting**: ML model may be overfit to historical data
3. **Lookback Bias**: Patterns found looking backward may not predict forward
4. **Data Quality**: API data may have errors/inconsistencies

### Philosophical Questions

1. **Mechanism**: WHY do patterns work? Psychology? Sociology? Random?
2. **Stability**: Will patterns persist as market matures?
3. **Universality**: Do patterns cross cultures/languages?
4. **Causality**: Does good name CAUSE success or do successful projects choose good names?
