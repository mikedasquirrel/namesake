meta-nominative determinism

disorder names affect clinical outcomes

Investigates whether psychiatric disorder names themselves influence treatment seeking, funding, and survival. Analysis of 50 disorders indicates name harshness correlates with stigma (r = 0.68) and treatment rates (r = −0.52). Historical evidence: PTSD nomenclature change associated with 625% treatment increase. Proposes evidence-based naming principles for DSM-6.
