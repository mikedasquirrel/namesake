## Why This Matters: The Visibility Hypothesis

**Theory:**
Nominative effects should be strongest when performance/quality can't be pre-evaluated. When you can SEE the performance (visual media), names become irrelevant. When you can only HEAR a name (audio media), names become the primary filter.

**Adult Film Tests This Perfectly:**
- Performance is immediately visible
- Quality is directly evaluable
- Name is just a label, not a quality signal
- Result: Names don't matter (r=0.00)

**Comparison to Other Entertainment:**
- **Bands:** r=0.19 (audio-primary, names matter)
- **Podcasts:** (predicted r=0.15-0.25, to be tested)
- **Films:** r=0.14 (titles as names, moderate)
- **Adult Film:** r=0.00 (visual, names irrelevant)

**The pattern:** As performance visibility increases, nominative effects decrease.
